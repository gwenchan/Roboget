----------------------


GetFile: 
		Link1=GetFile|http://www.imirin.com/files/36/Roboget/47/Roboget.zip|P
		
		Supported parameter: 
		|P for setting the PRIOR/Default Browser download
		|D for setting download in a DIRECT download
		|M for setting a Mirror

		|P for setting the PRIOR/Default Browser download
		|D for setting download in a DIRECT download
		|M for setting a Mirror
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
